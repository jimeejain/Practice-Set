WELCOME TO EASY SHOP MANAGER !

Easy Shop is a nationwide retail chain which has retail outlets in various cities of the country.

This application automates the retail processing. The application helps in faster billing and easy maintenance of the stock and customer details.

The application is used by the customers and the following user groups:

  1. Administrator
  2. Manager
  3. Billing Staff
